About Danito Café

Welcome to Danito Café, a place where passion for coffee meets a cozy and inspiring ambiance. We take pride in offering the finest coffee blends and an inviting space for you to work, network, and relax.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Danito Café was born out of a love for coffee and a desire to create a unique space where people can enjoy premium quality coffee and connect with others. Our founders, John Doe and Jane Smith, bring a wealth of experience in the hospitality industry and a deep appreciation for the art of coffee-making.

Professional Background

John Doe

Role: Co-Founder & Head Barista

Background: With over 15 years of experience in the coffee industry, John is a certified barista trainer and a coffee enthusiast. He has worked with some of the top coffee brands in the world and brings his expertise in coffee brewing and customer service to Danito Café.

Skills: Coffee brewing, latte art, customer service, staff training.

Education: Certified Barista Trainer, Specialty Coffee Association (SCA).

Employment: Former Head Barista at Brew & Co, Coffee Consultant at Bean Roasters.

Jane Smith

Role: Co-Founder & Operations Manager

Background: Jane has a background in business management and hospitality. She has managed several successful cafés and restaurants, bringing her operational expertise and attention to detail to Danito Café.

Skills: Business management, customer relations, inventory management, marketing.

Education: Bachelor's Degree in Business Management, University of London.

Employment: Former Operations Manager at Café Delight, Business Consultant at Hospitality Experts.

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainability and strive to minimize our environmental impact. From using eco-friendly packaging to supporting sustainable coffee farming practices, we are dedicated to making a positive difference for our planet and future generations.

Danito Foundation

We are proud supporters of the Costa Foundation, which aims to improve the lives of coffee-growing communities by providing access to education, healthcare, and clean water. We believe in giving back to the communities that make our coffee possible.

Policies and Reports

Our policies and reports outline our commitment to quality, sustainability, and ethical practices. We believe in transparency and accountability, ensuring that our customers can trust us to uphold the highest standards in everything we do.

Modern Slavery Statement

We are dedicated to preventing modern slavery and human trafficking in our operations and supply chains. Our Modern Slavery Statement outlines the steps we take to ensure that our business and suppliers adhere to ethical labor practices.

Our Mission

At Danito Café, our mission is to provide an exceptional coffee experience in a warm and welcoming environment. We are committed to sourcing the finest beans, delivering excellent customer service, and creating a space where our community can come together.
